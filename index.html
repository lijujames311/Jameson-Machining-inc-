Advanced Precision Grinding & Superfinishing

Tailored solutions in cylindrical, surface, belt grinding, and mirror-finish superfinishing — built for quality-driven industries.

Our Services

Cylindrical Grinding

We operate Zocca Como Italia machines, grinding up to 24" diameter × 80" length for shafts, rollers, and complex cylindrical components.

Surface Grinding

Precision flat surface grinding using our Chevalier FSG-3A-1020. Ideal for tool steel plates, mold bases, and critical fixtures.

FEIN GRIT Belt Grinding

Efficient and consistent belt grinding using GX75 2H system — delivering exceptional preparation and finishing of shafts and pipes.

Superfinishing

Mirror-quality superfinishing on critical rollers and shafts. Delivering ultra-smooth surfaces that meet the highest tolerance and finish standards.
