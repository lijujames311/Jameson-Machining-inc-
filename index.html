About Jameson Machining Inc.

I started Jameson Machining Inc. because I believe grinding should be done with absolute precision and efficiency, every time. I came to Canada as an international student in 2016, graduated from Sheridan College’s Mechanical Technician – Toolmaking program, and learned the art at Mississauga Jig Grinding. I built my skillset at Top Grade Molds (with supervisor Mike—his standards are my reference), Mold-Masters (ultra-precision, sub-3 micron tolerances), and Collins Aerospace (world-class requirements).

Today, Jameson is a young, ambitious shop. We do the job right, hold the tolerance, deliver on time, and support the future of Canadian manufacturing. Every customer gets real service and no-nonsense advice—if you need it ground, we’ll handle it.
