Our Services

Cylindrical Grinding

We operate Zocca Como Italia machines, grinding up to 24" diameter × 80" length for shafts, rollers, and complex cylindrical components.

Surface Grinding

Precision flat surface grinding using our Chevalier FSG-3A-1020. Ideal for tool steel plates, mold bases, and critical fixtures.

FEIN GRIT Belt Grinding

Efficient and consistent belt grinding using GX75 2H system — delivering exceptional preparation and finishing of shafts and pipes.

Superfinishing

Mirror-quality superfinishing on critical rollers and shafts. Delivering ultra-smooth surfaces that meet the highest tolerance and finish standards.

Precision Shaft Grinding – What Sets Us Apart

At Jameson Machining Inc., we specialize in long-shaft grinding for the most demanding applications. Whether it's aerospace, mold & die, or custom machinery, we deliver repeatable tolerances and mirror finishes on shafts up to 80 inches long and 24 inches in diameter.

Our grinding shop in Oakville is equipped with Zocca and John Shipman cylindrical grinders, Chevalier surface grinders, and FEIN GRIT centerless belt systems — giving us the flexibility to grind everything from hardened tool steels to coated rolls.

Holding roundness and straightness over long shafts takes skill, experience, and the right equipment. That’s where we excel — delivering precision results consistently across full lengths.

Need superfinishing? We go a step further with our 3M film-backed microfinishing system, achieving finishes down to Ra 2 µin.
