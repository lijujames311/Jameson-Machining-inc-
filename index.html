Grinding and Machining Services

Cylindrical & Shaft Grinding

OD/ID grinding up to 24” diameter and 80” long. Precision tolerances for shafts, bushings, rollers, and specialty jobs. Inspection and microfinishing available.

Surface Grinding

Flatness, parallelism, and surface finish—repeatable results for critical components. Custom fixturing for prototypes and production runs.

Centerless & Dedtru Grinding

Short runs, prototypes, and high-volume production—centerless grinding for pins, shafts, and more. Capacity for up to 500,000+ parts/year. Through-feed and plunge grinding available.

Chrome Plating & Repair

Full process: weld, chrome plate, pre-grind, final grind, and microfinish. Shaft repair and rebuild with trusted partners.

Custom Fixtures & Tooling

In-house fixture design for complex, odd, or high-mix jobs. Diamond and CBN wheels for every material. If it can be held, it can be ground.

Microfinishing

Superfinishing, film-backed abrasives, mirror surface improvement for new and repaired parts.

About Jameson Machining Inc.

Jameson Machining Inc. was founded on the belief that grinding should be handled with absolute precision, efficiency, and real accountability. After years working in some of Ontario’s top shops—from apprenticeship at Mississauga Jig Grinding, to Top Grade Molds (where industry-leading standards were learned and still followed today), through Mold-Masters and Collins Aerospace—Jameson Machining brings a next-generation approach to the GTA’s grinding needs.

As a young, future-focused shop, we serve customers who expect more: honest lead times, responsive communication, and consistent, quality results—whether it’s one-off or high-volume. Every job gets personal oversight. Our goal is to set a new standard for Canadian manufacturing.
