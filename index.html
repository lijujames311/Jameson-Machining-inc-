About Jameson Machining Inc.

Jameson Machining Inc. was founded on the belief that precision grinding should be done right the first time, every time.
Drawing on experience from top Ontario shops—Mississauga Jig Grinding, Top Grade Molds, Mold-Masters, and Collins Aerospace—Jameson Machining combines old-school skill with next-generation technology.
Our team is young, aggressive, and focused on supporting the future of Canadian manufacturing. Every project—whether a one-off or high-volume run—gets real service and results. We don’t do excuses. We do quality, reliability, and speed.
