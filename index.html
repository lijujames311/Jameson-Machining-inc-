Our Precision Services

We specialize in high-accuracy machining for industrial applications

Cylindrical Grinding

Precision grinding of shafts and cylindrical parts with tight tolerances

  • OD grinding up to 24" diameter
  • Precision tolerances to ±0.0001"
  • Various material capabilities

Surface Grinding

Creating perfectly flat surfaces with superior finish quality

  • Blanchard and precision surface grinding
  • Capacities up to 48" × 96"
  • Surface finishes to 8 RMS

Centerless Grinding

High-volume production grinding for consistent results

  • Through-feed and in-feed grinding
  • Diameter range: 0.030" to 4"
  • Lengths up to 20 feet

Microfinishing

Superfinishing for superior surface quality and precision

  • Surface finishes to 2 RMS
  • Tolerance capabilities to ±0.00005"
  • Specialized for bearing surfaces

Chrome Shaft Operations

Specialized services for chrome-plated shafts

  • Pre and post-grinding operations
  • Chrome plating coordination
  • Precision straightening

Welding Services

Specialized welding for industrial applications

  • MIG, TIG, and stick welding
  • Hardfacing and buildup
  • Precision welding for repairs
