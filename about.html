About Jameson Machining Inc.

Since 2022, we’ve been delivering precision machining solutions from our base in Oakville, GTA (1421 Speers Rd, Oakville, ON L6L2X5). We specialize in cylindrical, centerless, and surface grinding, welding, chrome plating with repair, lead coating, micro-finishing, heat treatment facilitation, and assembly.
